Our Story

Where Coffee
Meets Chrome

Motown Stories Cafe is not just a place to grab a coffee — it's a full throttle experience. Born from a love of classic cars, vintage motorcycles, and soul music, we built a space that feels like stepping into a 1960s Hollywood garage that somehow also makes the best espresso in town.

From the exposed brick walls covered in motorcycle art to the Harley parked by the bar — every corner has a story. Come in, slow down, and find yours.

The Vibe

More Than
a Cafe

01

Specialty Coffee

From Kopi Luwak to Syphon brews, our bar is stocked with the world's finest coffees brewed with intention. Black, white, or on the rocks — we do it properly.

02

Iron & Ink Interiors

Exposed brick walls, motorcycle murals, vintage frames, a real Harley parked by the bar. Every detail is a nod to the golden era of motors and music.

03

Drive It Your Way

Grab a coffee, grab the keys. From everyday hatchbacks to exotic supercars — we're the only cafe where the ride is also on the menu.
