Our Story

Where Coffee
Meets Chrome

Motown Stories Cafe is not just a place to grab a coffee — it's a full throttle experience. Born from a love of classic cars, vintage motorcycles, and soul music, we built a space that feels like stepping into a 1960s Hollywood garage that somehow also makes the best espresso in town.

From the exposed brick walls covered in motorcycle art to the Harley parked by the bar — every corner has a story. Come in, slow down, and find yours.
